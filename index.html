Welcome To Vishal Paints
Vishal Paints was founded in 1994 by Arun Lanjewar who has vast experience in the field of Paint Technology.
With his vision as a driving force ,Vishal Paints is now a Leader and Largest manufacturer of Industrial Specialty Paints in Central India.We have a client base spread over all corners of the country.

Vishal Paints has a wide Spectrum of High Performance Industrial and Decorative Coatings.
Topnotch Quality, Reliability and Perfection at all cost are hallmark of its products, making them truly world class.
Right from inception, Vishal Paints has been doing pioneering work in the development of new applications and seeking innovative solutions.

It has taken up numerous areas, identified the problems and evolved perfect products to ensure long lasting solutions.
Unconventional techniques developed by Vishal Paints are admired by various industries, providing them with tailor made solutions.

The company has well-equipped Quality Control and Development Laboratory.
Sophisticated Testing Instruments like Spectrophotometer (Color Matching Computer) has earned the company the reputation as a trustworthy supplier for delivering consistent shades, batch after batch.
Vishal Paints has a State of the Art Infrastructure to ensure top quality as well as fast deliveries for small quantites to huge volumes.
Few of the customized solutions we have successfully provided during last few years are development of Non Toxic Paints(lead free Paints) for Mattel Toys, Development of Environment friendly Low VOC  Paints, Cool Paint which reduces temperature of the dwellings by 6 °C and many more such innovative products.
